How to Use
  1. Log in to VTOP and go to the course registration page
  2. The scraper automatically intercepts network data as pages load
  3. A floating panel will appear on the VTOP page with live stats
  4. Click Export JSON/CSV here or from the panel
  5. Import the JSON into FFCS Planner or open it directly
